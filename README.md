#Anonymized Fraud & Identity-Abuse Forensic Analysis
Overview

This repository contains a forensic case study focused on anonymized incidents of fraud and identity abuse. The analysis demonstrates methodology, evidence handling, and investigative processes typically used in digital forensics.

Contents
PDF Report – Executive summary, scope, methodology, findings, and recommendations.
Evidence Artifacts – Hashes (SHA-256) and anonymized screenshots or logs.
Timeline & Indicators – Chronology of events and indicators of compromise.
Objectives
Document a real-world forensic investigation while preserving anonymity.
Showcase professional skills in data analysis, incident response, and reporting.
Provide a structured case study for academic or professional purposes.
Methodology
Collection of anonymized digital evidence.
Verification and hashing of data artifacts.
Analysis using forensic tools and techniques.
Timeline reconstruction and identification of compromise indicators.
Compilation of findings and actionable recommendations.
